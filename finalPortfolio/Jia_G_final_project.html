FINAL PROJECT WRITEUP:

I had a lot of fun creating brain art in our first

assignment so I wanted to take a festive

approach to it this time. It is also an

extension of my first two alpha wave

assignments as well. I was inspired by the

Filipino Christmas decor called a ‚Äúparol‚Äù for

this one. So I started with alpha waves on

brainimation and went from there. It was

hard to get the main star exactly sharp and

look like a traditional star. For many trials it

looked like a flower. It still has the illusion and movement of alpha waves but I still wanted

the lights and colours to look like a traditional parol.

The design features a prominent, sharply defined

5-pointed star in the center, with its tip pointing

straight up for a traditional look. This main star is

surrounded by two rings of smaller, equally sharp

stars that dynamically move and pulse, adding to

the festive feel. With smaller stars all around the

main star just to make it look more exciting and

mimic a true parol. All these elements are

encompassed within a larger, soft circular glow that

expands and contracts, ensuring the smaller stars

remain within its radiant embrace. The entire design glows and moves in a cohesive, organic

manner, creating a vibrant and authentic parol-inspired animation.
