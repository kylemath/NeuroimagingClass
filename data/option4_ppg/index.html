üéØ Your Task

Develop an algorithm to estimate blood oxygen saturation (SpO‚ÇÇ) from the raw PPG signals and validate your estimates against the reference values provided.

Expected Approach:

  1. Load and visualize the raw PPG data
  2. Apply bandpass filtering (0.5-5 Hz) to isolate cardiac signal
  3. Detect cardiac cycles using peak detection
  4. Calculate AC and DC components for red and infrared channels
  5. Compute R-ratio: R = (AC_red/DC_red) / (AC_ir/DC_ir)
  6. Estimate SpO2 using: SpO2 ‚âà 110 - 25 √ó R
  7. Compare your estimates to reference values
