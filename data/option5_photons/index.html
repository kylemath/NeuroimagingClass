üéØ Your Task

Analyze the data to test whether mental effort produces detectable changes in photon emission. Critical evaluation required!

Required Analysis Steps:

  1. Visualize raw photon counts over time for each condition
  2. Calculate mean photon rate per condition (expected: ~42-43 Hz)
  3. Statistical testing: compare each condition to control baseline
  4. Apply multiple comparison corrections
  5. Test for temperature drift (linear trend analysis)
  6. Assess replication (compare two mental math sessions)

Critical Discussion (REQUIRED):

  • Is the effect statistically significant after corrections?
  • Could temperature drift explain the results?
  • Could movement artifacts explain the results?
  • Could statistical noise (Type I error) explain the results?
  • What additional controls would strengthen conclusions?
  • Is the effect size plausible given known biophysics?
