‚ú® Mental Effort & Photon Emission

Investigate whether mental effort affects photon detection on the forehead using ultra-sensitive light detection in complete darkness. Design a controlled experiment comparing photon counts during different mental states: rest, mental math, visualization, and meditation. Critical thinking is essential ‚Äî carefully control for artifacts like movement, temperature changes, and ambient light leaks. This project is about rigorous experimental design and skeptical analysis, not confirming a hypothesis.

  3. Short report (3-5 pages) with skeptical discussion
  4. Proposed improvements for future experiments
